Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect:
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Seoul Mate apps(android and iOS).
1. Name
2. Email address
3. Facebook Profile Photo
4. Locale Information
5. User friends on Facebook
In addition we also collect analytics of the app through Google Analytics framework for iOS and Android.

The Way We Use Information:
We use the information you provide about yourself in app only when you post or like/comment on a post. We do not share this information with outside parties.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information
We use Facebook login for accessing your personal information, which you have declared public. You can always change your name, profile photo, or locale in your Facebook profile and it will be updated on our servers. You can also revoke access by removing Seoul Mate app from your app settings on Facebook.

How To Contact Us
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at seoulmate.me@gmail.com.

About us

Seoul Mate is an information and resource sharing app for International Students in South Korea.Seoul Mate lets you post your question, find friends from your university, and get updates about jobs, internships, scholarships and more. Seoul Mate is the perfect way to keep up with your friends in South Korea. It also provides you with campus maps so that you don't get lost on first day at your university.
